Common Signs You Need Trenchless Repair

Recognizing the early signs of sewer line issues can save you time and money. Here are some red flags to watch for:

  • Unpleasant odors near your yard or home.
  • Slow drains and frequent clogs.
  • Soggy areas in your yard, especially near your sewer line.
  • Unexplained increases in your water bill.

If you notice any of these signs, contact a licensed plumber right away. Early intervention can prevent further damage and save you money.
