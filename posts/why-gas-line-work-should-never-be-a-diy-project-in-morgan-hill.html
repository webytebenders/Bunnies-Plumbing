Understanding Gas Line Systems

Gas lines are critical components of your home, supplying fuel for heating, cooking, and hot water. However, these lines are also under significant pressure and can be hazardous if not handled correctly. Here are some key reasons why gas line work is complex:

  • Pressure Management: Gas lines operate under high pressure, making even minor leaks potentially dangerous.
  • Detection of Leaks: Gas leaks can be hard to detect. Often, a rotten egg smell indicates a leak, but relying solely on your sense of smell is risky.
  • Regulations and Codes: Local building codes in Morgan Hill require specific standards for gas line installation and repair, which a professional plumber is trained to follow.
