What is Trenchless Sewer Repair?

Trenchless sewer repair is a modern technique that allows plumbers to repair or replace damaged sewer lines without extensive excavation. Here are the two primary methods we use:

  1. Pipe Bursting: This method involves breaking apart the existing damaged pipe while simultaneously installing a new pipe in its place. It is ideal for severely damaged pipes that need complete replacement.
  2. CIPP Lining: Cured-in-place pipe (CIPP) lining is a method where we insert a resin-saturated liner into the damaged pipe, which is then cured to create a new pipe within the old one. This method is excellent for pipes that are still structurally sound but need reinforcement.

Signs You May Need Trenchless Sewer Repair

If you notice any of the following signs, it may be time to consider trenchless sewer repair:

  • Frequent backups or slow drains in your home.
  • Unpleasant odors coming from your drains.
  • Soggy patches in your yard or landscape where the sewer line runs.
  • Cracks in your foundation or walls.

If you observe any of these symptoms, contact a licensed plumber right away to avoid further damage.

The Benefits of Choosing Trenchless Technology

Homeowners in Morgan Hill and the broader Bay Area choose trenchless technology for several reasons:

  • Cost-Effective: Although the initial investment can be more than traditional methods, the overall costs are lower due to reduced labor and restoration expenses.
  • Time-Saving: Trenchless repairs can often be completed in a single day, while traditional repairs may take days or weeks.
  • Environmentally Friendly: The no-dig approach means less impact on your landscaping and less debris to dispose of.
