What Causes Sewer Line Damage?

Sewer lines can suffer from various issues, including:

  • Tree Roots: Roots from nearby trees can infiltrate sewer lines, causing blockages or breaks.
  • Corrosion: Older pipes may corrode over time, leading to leaks and breaks.
  • Improper Installation: Poorly installed pipes can lead to serious structural issues.
  • Ground Shifts: Soil movement can cause pipes to crack or misalign.
