Understanding Our Services

At Bunnies Plumbing, we offer a wide array of plumbing services designed to meet the diverse needs of Bay Area homeowners. Here’s a closer look at some of our key offerings:

Trenchless Sewer Repair

Traditional sewer repair can be invasive and costly, often requiring significant excavation. Instead, we utilize trenchless technology to repair damaged sewer lines with minimal digging. Techniques like pipe bursting and CIPP lining allow us to replace or repair pipes without tearing up your yard, saving you time and money.

Drain Cleaning & Hydro Jetting

Regular maintenance of your drains can prevent severe clogs and backups. Our drain cleaning services, including hydro jetting, effectively clear out debris and buildup, ensuring your plumbing system operates smoothly. If you notice slow drains or persistent odors, it's time to contact a licensed plumber.

24/7 Emergency Plumbing

Plumbing emergencies can happen at any time, which is why we offer 24/7 emergency services. Whether it's a burst pipe, a sewage backup, or a gas leak, our team is ready to respond quickly and mitigate damage to your home.

The Importance of Professional Plumbing Services

Many homeowners may consider DIY solutions for plumbing issues, but some problems require professional intervention. Here are a few scenarios where calling a plumber is essential:

  • Persistent Clogs: If you’ve tried store-bought drain cleaners without success, it’s time to call in the professionals. Many times, these products can worsen the issue, leading to more significant problems.
  • Water Leaks: Hidden water leaks can cause extensive damage if not addressed promptly. If you notice damp spots or increased water bills, it’s best to schedule an inspection.
  • Gas Line Issues: If you smell rotten eggs near your gas appliances, it’s crucial to evacuate the area and call a licensed plumber immediately.
