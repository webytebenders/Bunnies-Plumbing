Who We Are

A Family-Oriented Plumbing Company Built on Trust, Skill, and Hard Work

Led by Adhemar Castaneda, Bunnies Plumbing & Trenchless Technology has become one of the most trusted plumbing companies in the Bay Area.

With over 20 years of utility contracting experience, Adhemar built this company from the ground up with a simple philosophy: treat every customer's home like your own. Based in Morgan Hill, CA, we serve homeowners and businesses throughout the entire South Bay.

What sets us apart is our dedication to trenchless technology — a modern, minimally invasive approach to sewer line repair and replacement that saves your yard, your trees, and your wallet. We were among the first in the region to specialize in this method, and we've perfected it over hundreds of successful projects.

From a simple drain cleaning to a full sewer line replacement, we bring the same level of care, professionalism, and transparency to every single job.
