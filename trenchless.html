What is Trenchless Technology?

A revolutionary approach that replaces sewer lines without digging up your property

20+ Years Experience

Trenchless sewer repair is a modern plumbing method that allows us to repair or replace underground pipes without excavating large trenches through your yard, driveway, or landscaping. Instead of digging up your entire property, we access the damaged pipe through one or two small entry points and complete the work underground.

At Bunnies Plumbing, this isn't just a service we offer — it's our specialty. With 20+ years of experience and hundreds of successful projects, we deliver faster, cleaner, and more affordable results.

Our Trenchless Methods

Two proven techniques for different situations

Pipe Bursting

For Full Pipe Replacement

A hydraulic machine pulls a new, larger pipe through the existing damaged pipe, breaking apart the old pipe outward as the new one takes its place.

  • Severely damaged or collapsed pipes
  • Old clay, cast iron, or Orangeburg pipes
  • Pipes with multiple breaks or bellies
  • Upgrading to a larger pipe diameter

Pipe Lining (CIPP)

For Pipe Rehabilitation

A resin-coated flexible liner is inserted into the existing pipe and inflated, creating a new pipe-within-a-pipe that cures in place and seals all cracks and joints.

  • Pipes with cracks or small breaks
  • Leaking pipe joints
  • Root intrusion prevention
  • Pipes under buildings or driveways

  • Trees & driveway fully preserved

Our Trenchless Process

From inspection to completion, here's what to expect

1

Camera Inspection

We send an HD camera through your sewer line to identify the exact location and nature of the problem.

2

Free Estimate

We provide a detailed, upfront estimate with no hidden fees. We explain your options clearly.

3

Preparation

We dig small access points (typically just 2) at each end of the damaged section. Your yard stays intact.

4

Trenchless Repair

Using pipe bursting or pipe lining, we replace or repair the damaged sewer line underground.

5

Final Inspection

Another camera inspection confirms the repair is perfect. We backfill the access points and clean up.
